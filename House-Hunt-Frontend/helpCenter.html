Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my property?

You can list your property under the “List Your Property” tab. Fill in the required details like rent, BHK type, photos, and location.

Is HouseHunt completely brokerage-free?

Yes! HouseHunt ensures direct communication between tenants and landlords. No brokers or hidden charges.

How can tenants contact landlords?

Tenants can message landlords directly after finding a property they are interested in.

Are the listings verified?

Every listing is checked by our system and team to ensure accurate and trustworthy information.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the “Forgot Password” feature on the login page to reset your password securely.
